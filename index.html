About Me

I am in my final year as a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis. As a graduate research assistant at the Visual Interface and Behavior Exploration Lab (VIBE), I am interested in investigating visualization literacy, understanding perceived trust in data visualizations, and leveraging AI/ML techniques to help users understand data and data visualizations.

👨‍🏫 Teaching Experience - 6.67/7

Fall 2024 📍 Introduction to Visualization

Grateful to my advisor Dr. Alvitta Ottley for this incredible teaching experience! I scored 6.67/7 (Department Average: 6.11/7, School Average: 6.18/7) for co-instructing "Introduction to Visualization."

My teaching journey started during undergrad with several TA experiences in Computer Science, Physics, and Mathematics courses. I genuinely love helping students - often extending sessions when needed and learning alongside them. Students have been an incredible source of learning and guidance for me.

This time, I created course materials, facilitated interactive sessions, and taught data visualization including data wrangling, D3.js, design guidelines, what makes good vs. bad & ugly visualizations, perception, prototyping, and helped students complete final projects creating interactive visualization dashboards for social good 🌍💧🏭.

I'm always looking to improve my teaching and make myself available anytime (sometimes even at midnight!) to help students succeed. The best moment was when I was waiting for chai ☕️ at a campus cafe, and a student approached me to thank me for the course - they said it was their favorite and even helped them land an internship using their final project! ✨
